# WOVENWARP Visual Asset Manifest

This document defines the production visual system for WOVENWARP across the website, catalog, product pages, social campaigns, and future video work.

## Global Art Direction

- Mood: quiet luxury, warm minimalism, boutique hospitality, editorial calm.
- Palette: travertine, sand, limestone, walnut, clay, warm ivory, natural wool, matte black.
- Lighting: natural sunlight, soft shadows, warm ambient contrast, gentle highlights on texture.
- Composition: architectural framing, negative space, visible rug texture, refined furniture styling.
- Avoid: bright commercial lighting, oversaturated color, busy interiors, cheap props, excessive decoration, generic e-commerce styling.

## Priority 1 Assets

### Hero Visuals

Purpose: first-impression luxury storytelling.

Requirements:
- Architectural interiors, luxury living rooms, organic modern homes.
- Soft sunlight, calm neutral palettes, editorial composition.
- Full rug visibility, premium furniture styling, visible texture.
- Negative space for typography.

Recommended formats:
- Desktop hero: 2400x1400 or wider.
- Mobile hero: 1200x1600 crop.
- Keep subject clear on the right or center-right when left-side typography is used.

### Lifestyle Interiors

Purpose: aspiration, emotional luxury, interior design credibility.

Scenes:
- Premium living room.
- Bedroom styling.
- Lounge space.
- Reading corner.
- Luxury office.
- Boutique hotel suite or lobby.

Direction:
- Rugs should feel integrated into lived architectural environments.
- Avoid catalog-like staging.

### Texture & Macro Visuals

Purpose: tactile perception and luxury material storytelling.

Capture:
- Fibres, knots, handweaving details, wool texture, silk reflection, pile variations.
- Grazing light across raised surfaces.
- Shallow depth of field with enough detail for zoomed storytelling.

### Craftsmanship Visuals

Purpose: authenticity, process, emotional value.

Capture:
- Hand knotting, loom process, thread work, washing, drying, finishing, trimming.
- Natural hands-at-work framing.
- Editorial documentary tone, not commercial stock styling.

### Product Cutouts

Purpose: product pages, catalog views, zoom detail, Shopify/WooCommerce readiness.

Required angles:
- Flat front view.
- Folded corner.
- Side texture.
- Rolled rug.
- Edge finishing.
- Backside weaving.

Direction:
- Clean background, accurate color, no dramatic shadows that hide edges.
- Keep one consistent crop system for all products.

## Priority 2 Assets

### Artisan Portraits

Purpose: humanize heritage and craft.

Style:
- Documentary/editorial.
- Natural lighting.
- Authentic emotion.
- Hands working or portrait with loom.
- Avoid overly commercial styling.

### Material Story Visuals

Purpose: communicate material quality.

Capture:
- Raw wool, natural fibres, yarn bundles, dyed threads, fabric layers, texture arrangements.
- Use soft daylight and calm neutral styling.

### Collection Banners

Purpose: collection landing pages and large editorial transitions.

Collections:
- Organic Modern.
- Sculptural Texture.
- Vintage Artistry.
- Minimal Contemporary.
- Hospitality Collection.

Format:
- Ultra-wide cinematic compositions, ideally 2400x1000 or 3000x1200.
- Leave negative space for headings and short collection copy.

## Priority 3 Assets

Examples:

- Texture layering.
- Soft shadows.
- Architectural corners.

### About / Heritage Visuals

Purpose: trust, legacy, and authenticity.

Include:

- Weaving process.
- Family craftsmanship.
- Archives.

### Designer / Studio Visuals

Purpose: show creative process and design direction.

Include:
- Moodboards.
- Sketches.

### Social Media / Editorial Visuals

Purpose: Instagram, Pinterest, advertising campaigns, editorial blogs, PR.

Direction:
- Campaign-like, atmospheric, warm, and textural.
- Reuse website art direction so the brand stays visually consistent.

Recommended motion content:
- Loom weaving sequences.
- Slow-motion texture visuals.
- Fabric movement.
- Artisan process storytelling.
- Interior cinematic pans.
- Macro texture motion.
- Sunlight movement across interiors and rug pile.
